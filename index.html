My Trip to Jamaica

In June of last year, I took a trip to Montego Bay, Jamaica, with 11 of my closest friends. We went there to celebrate another friend's 35th birthday. Originally, the trip was planned for June 2020, but Covid-19 had other plans.

Villa

We stayed at the Golden Castle villa in Rose Hall, a plantation in Montego Bay, Jamaica. The villa accomadated 16 guests and came with 12 bedrooms, in additon to a home theater, game room and fitness, spa and sauna facilities. Included in our stay were a butler, chef, wait staff, gardner, etc.

Private Sailing

One of my favorite memories from this trip was the private sailing. Because my friends like to coordinate looks on trips, we had a neon swimwear theme on the boat. We laughed and sipped countless bottles of champagne, while sailing the Caribbean Sea. The boat attendants danced with us and served us more drinks, then served us jerk chicken. It was amazing!

River Rafting

I was so excited to ride down the river,on a beautiful handcrafted bamboo raft. During our river journey,we got to experience a natural body rub, using limestone paste.
